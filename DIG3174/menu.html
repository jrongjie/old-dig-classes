HOT DRINKS

Mocha Cocoa Hot Chocolate
A sweet, mild hot chocolate. Contains unsweetened cocoa powder, mocha sauce, coffee powder, salt, maple syrup, semi-sweet chocolate, sugar, milk, half and half, and vanilla extract
Sinister Aztec Hot Chocolate
A spicy, hot chocolate with a hint of dark chocolate. Contains cinnamon, vanilla, a pinch of salt, and half a chili pepper.
Optional: 2 shots of brandy or Grand Marnier.
Volcan's Vegan Hot Chocolate
A refreshing sip of unsweetened almond milk, unsweetened cocoa powder, Dutch cocoa, dairy-free chocolate, sugar, peppermint extract, topped off with coconut whipped cream

COLD DRINKS

Créme de la Cocoa
A thick, creamy chocolate mixture enjoyed with a spoon or crackers. Contains vanilla, sugar, sour cream, heavy cream, gelatin, semi-sweet chocolate, white chocolate, milk, eggs, and confectioner's sugar
Frozen Cocoa
Hot days got nothing on this simple frozen drink. Contains milk, creamer, chocolate syrup, ice, vanilla ice cream
Optional: Can substitute with other flavors
Monday Chocolate Melt
Start off your week with a refreshing, mildly sweet iced hot chocolate. Contains unsweetened cocoa powder, mocha sauce, coffee powder, salt, semi-sweet chocolate, sugar, milk, creamer, and vanilla extract
