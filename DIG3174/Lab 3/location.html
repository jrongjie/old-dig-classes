How to Find Edzel's

We're conveniently located just off I-π. A short 7 hour drive from Miami, 5 hours from Orlando, 3 hours from Tallahassee, and 6 hours from Pensacola. Take exit 347̃ and you'll see a dirt road on the right. Follow that for about a mile, then take the next 19 right turns. You should see a woman with a fruit stand. Buy 2 blueberries and a single Gala apple. Once this transaction is complete, the woman will take you into the woods. Follow her for about 30-45 minutes. It might seem like she's walking around in circles, but trust her. When you emerge from the forest (ideally after having a life changing epiphany), you'll be right on our doorstep. Please don't try to find us with Google Maps. The insist that Falske isn't a real place, and they additionally insist that Edzel's Invisible Oddities is not a real thing. You're on the website, so obviously we're real.
